Welcome!

The Monroe Chess Club is dedicated to promoting chess across North East Louisiana, and helping chess enthusiasts meet new players.

Join Us!

Where to Find Us

We meet weekly on Monday evenings at 5:30 PM at Enochs Pub and Grill and Saturday mornings at 10:00 AM at the Books-A-Million in Monroe.

All ages and skill levels are welcome!

What to Expect

Casual Chess. Friendly Conversations.

Join the Conversation

Join our Facebook group and become part of the conversation. We share upcoming tournaments, puzzles, and chess related topics so you can stay up-to-date.

FAQ

  • Do I need to let the club know before I show up for the first time?
    No. Anytime the club is meeting, you are welcome to stop by at your convenience.
  • How do I know what's going on at the club this week?
    Please see our Facebook Group for events and updates.
  • Do I need to bring my own chess set and/or clock?
    Bringing your own set and clock is encouraged, but not required.
  • Are beginners welcome?
    Yes! Everyone is welcome, all skill levels and ages.
